Banana Bread
Tray Type: Loaf Pan       Servings: 12 slices       Bake Time: 50-65 minutes       Prep Time: 30 minutes
Dry Ingredients
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup light brown sugar, packed
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder, heaping
  • 1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 3/4 cup of add-ins (chocolate chips, walnuts, pecans, etc.), optional
Wet Ingredients
  • 2 eggs, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup (8 tbsp or 1 stick) unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
  • 1/3 cup sour cream, room temperature (full-fat plain or greek yogurt works too)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 bananas, mashed
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 350F and line and grease a loaf pan
  2. With an electric mixer, cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy, about 4 minutes
  3. Add the eggs in one at a time and beat just until incorporated
  4. Whisk in the mashed bananas, vanilla, and sour cream well
  5. Sift in the flour, salt, baking soda, cinnamon, and nutmeg
  6. Fold in add-ins if desired, be careful not to overmix
  7. Pour the batter into your loaf pan and bake for 50-65 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean. At the 30 minute mark, cover the loaf with foil to avoid a burnt topping
